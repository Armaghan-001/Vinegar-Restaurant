Michelin Star

Michelin Guide · 2023

Vinegar Food Restaurant is honoured with three Michelin stars for its uncompromising commitment to culinary artistry. Our tasting menus reflect decades of refinement, using only the finest seasonal ingredients. The intimate dining experience is orchestrated by Chef de Cuisine with flawless precision.

This recognition places us among an elite group of restaurants worldwide, celebrating innovation and respect for tradition.

World's 50 Best

Restaurant Academy · 2024

Ranked #12 in the World's 50 Best Restaurants, Vinegar Food stands as a beacon of contemporary gastronomy. Voted by an international academy of chefs and critics, this award highlights our distinctive flavour philosophy and exceptional service.

Our position on the list reflects growing global appreciation for our signature dishes and wine program.

Chef of the Year

National Restaurant Awards · 2023

Executive Chef Isabella Rossi was named National Chef of the Year for her revolutionary approach to flavour pairing and dedication to mentorship. Her tasting menus at Vinegar Food have become a benchmark for emerging chefs.

Chef Rossi's philosophy combines local heritage with modern techniques, earning widespread acclaim.

Best Wine Program

Wine Spectator · 2022

Wine Spectator's Grand Award honours our cellar of over 2,500 labels from prestigious vineyards. Our sommelier team designs pairings that elevate every dish, with rare vintages and boutique producers.

The program includes exclusive wine dinners and vineyard immersion experiences for guests.

Luxury Restaurant Award

Intl. Hospitality Institute · 2024

Recognized for the most exquisite fine-dining interior and guest experience worldwide. From handcrafted tableware to ambient lighting and personalised service, every detail exudes elegance.

Vinegar Food sets the standard for luxury hospitality, creating unforgettable moments for discerning patrons.

Sustainable Kitchen

Green Culinary Awards · 2023

Pioneering zero-waste fine dining, we were honoured with the Sustainable Kitchen Award for our closed-loop system. We source from local farms, compost waste, and upcycle ingredients creatively.

Our rooftop garden supplies fresh herbs and microgreens, reducing our carbon footprint while enhancing flavour.
